# Netlify Deployment Guide | Pure Stone Vibes

This guide explains how to get your handcrafted gemstone portfolio live on the web using **Netlify**.

## Option 1: The "Manual" Way (Fastest)

This is the easiest way to see your site live immediately without any complex setup.

1.  **Prepare your Folder**: Ensure all your files (`index.html`, `gallery.html`, `namedpics/`, etc.) are in one main folder on your desktop.
2.  **Log In**: Go to [Netlify.com](https://www.netlify.com/) and create a free account.
3.  **Drag & Drop**:
    *   Once logged in, go to the **Sites** tab.
    *   Scroll down to the bottom where it says "Want to deploy a new site without connecting to Git?"
    *   **Drag your entire project folder** into the dashed box.
4.  **Live!**: Netlify will give you a random URL (like `shiny-gem-123.netlify.app`). Your site is now live!

---

## Option 2: The "Professional" Way (Best for Updates)

If you use GitHub, Netlify will automatically update your site every time we make a change.

1.  **Connect GitHub**: In the Netlify dashboard, click **Add new site** > **Import an existing project**.
2.  **Select Repository**: Choose your "Pure Stone Vibes" project from your GitHub account.
3.  **Deploy**: Click **Deploy site**. From now on, whenever I help you update the code, the website will refresh automatically.

---

## Managing Your Inquiries

Since we added **Netlify Forms**, here is how you find your messages:

1.  Go to your site dashboard in Netlify.
2.  Click on the **Forms** tab at the top.
3.  Click on the form named **"inquiry"**.
4.  You will see all submissions there. 
5.  **Pro Tip**: In the "Form notification" settings, you can add your email address so Netlify emails you the second a customer reaches out!

---

## Custom Domain
If you ever want to change the URL to something like `PureStoneVibes.com`, you can do that in the **Domain Management** section of your site settings. ✦
